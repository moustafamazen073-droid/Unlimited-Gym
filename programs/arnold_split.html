🏆 ARNOLD SPLIT

🔥 Who is it for? Intermediate to advanced lifters. Combines opposing muscle groups for maximum pump and intensity.

📅 Training Frequency: 6 days/week

📌 DAY 1: CHEST & BACK

Exercise Sets Reps
Incline Dumbbell Press 3 6-10
Pull-ups 3 6-10
Chest Flyes 3 8-12
Barbell Rows 3 8-10
Dips 2 max
Seated Rows 3 8-10

📌 DAY 2: SHOULDERS & ARMS

Exercise Sets Reps
Shoulder Press 3 8-12
Incline Dumbbell Curls 3 8-12
Lateral Raises 3 12-15
Overhead Tricep Extension 4 8-12
Rear Delt Flyes 3 8-10
Hammer Curls 3 8-10
Tricep Pushdowns 3 8-12

📌 DAY 3: LEGS

Exercise Sets Reps
Calf Raises 3 12-15
Squats 3 8-12
Leg Press 3 8-10
Leg Extensions 3 8-12
Leg Curls 3 10-12

📌 DAY 4: CHEST & BACK

Exercise Sets Reps
Dumbbell Press 3 6-10
Lat Pulldowns 3 6-8
Dips 3 10-12
T-Bar Rows 3 10-12
Push-ups 3 Max

📌 DAY 5: SHOULDERS & ARMS

Exercise Sets Reps
Arnold Press 3 6-8
Preacher Curls 3 6-10
Front Raises 3 12-15
Tricep Dips 3 10-15
Shrugs 3 12-15

📌 DAY 6: LEGS

Exercise Sets Reps
Front Squats 3 8-10
Romanian Deadlifts 3 10-12
Walking Lunges 3 10 (each leg)
Hack Squats 3 10-12
Seated Calf Raises 3 15-20

⭐ Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets | 2-3 minutes between exercises
