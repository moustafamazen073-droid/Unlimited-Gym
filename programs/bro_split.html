💪 BRO SPLIT

🔥 Who is it for? Perfect for beginners and intermediates. Focus on one muscle group per day for maximum volume and recovery.

📅 Training Frequency: 5-6 days/week

📌 DAY 1: CHEST

Exercise Sets Reps
Barbell Bench Press 3 8-12
Incline Dumbbell Press 3 8-10
Chest Flyes 3 12-15
Dips 3 10-15

📌 DAY 2: BACK

Exercise Sets Reps
Lat Pulldowns 3 8-12
Barbell Rows 4 8-12
Seated Rows 3 10-12
Face Pulls 3 6-10
Pull-ups 3 8-12

📌 DAY 3: SHOULDERS

Exercise Sets Reps
Shoulder Press 3 6-10
Lateral Raises 3 10-12
Front Raises 3 8-12
Rear Delt Flyes 3 8-12
Shrugs 3 12-15

📌 DAY 4: LEGS

Exercise Sets Reps
Squats 3 8-12
Leg Press 3 8-12
Leg Extensions 3 8-12
Leg Curls 3 10-12
Calf Raises 3 10-15
Lunges 3 10-12 (each leg)

📌 DAY 5: ARMS

Exercise Sets Reps
Barbell Curls 4 8-12
Overhead Tricep Extension 3 8-12
Incline Dumbbell Curls 3 8-12
Tricep Pushdowns 3 8-12
Hammer Curls 3 8-12
Concentration Curls 3 8-12

📌 DAY 6: OPTIONAL - WEAK POINTS / CARDIO

Exercise Sets Reps
Focus on Lagging Muscles 3-4 10-15
HIIT Cardio 15-20 min —

⭐ Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets | 2 minutes between exercises

📝 Note: Rest day after Day 5 or Day 6 depending on recovery
