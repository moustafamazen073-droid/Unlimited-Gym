About Us

Welcome to Unlimited Gym – Alexandria

Most gyms are just places with machines and weights, but Unlimited Gym is truly different. While other gyms focus only on routines and schedules, we focus on creating a community where passion, dedication, and achievement come together. Here, you don't just train—you grow, you learn, and you become part of something bigger.

Our gym stands out for its inspiring environment, expert coaching, and state-of-the-art facilities, making every workout meaningful and every goal achievable.

Our Story

Unlimited Gym started as a simple, empty space, nothing more than four walls. It was in this very space that Ahmed Abdel-Moneim, the youngest of Ibrahim Abdel-Moneim's sons, began training alone. With dedication, consistency, and a love for the sport, Ahmed achieved what many only dream of — he became a World Karate Champion.

Inspired by Ahmed's incredible journey, Ibrahim and his sons Islam, Ahmed, and Mohamed decided to transform this empty space into Unlimited Gym, a place where talent, discipline, and ambition meet opportunity. Over the years, we have welcomed many world-class champions and elite athletes, creating a hub where champions train, mentor others, and inspire the next generation.

On another front, we have expanded our horizons to host prominent figures from around the world. Over many long years, our facility has welcomed contestants of the Miss Eco International pageant, arriving from dozens of different countries. This makes our institution a unique place that not only celebrates and nurtures exceptional athletic talent but also honors diverse forms of excellence on a global level.

Why Unlimited Gym is Different

  • Professional Coaching for Every Level: Whether you're a beginner or an elite athlete, our certified trainers guide you through every step of your journey.
  • Inspiring Champion Community: Train alongside world-class athletes and champions who motivate and push you to achieve your personal best.

  • A Place Where Stories Begin: Many champions started here, and many more will continue to grow. From local talent to world-class athletes, Unlimited Gym is where hard work turns into success.
